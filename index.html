Get Started with PSU-GPT Today!

Unlock the full potential of university resources and information within the PSU community, as you embark on a cutting-edge and efficient journey. Interact effortlessly with PSU-GPT in just three simple steps:

  • Open PSU-GPT

    To get started, simply click on the link https://beta.psu-gpt.com , this will launch our powerful AI chatbot, PSU-GPT, that is ready to assist you with any questions or discussions you have in mind. Our Embark on an exceptional interactive journey, designed to deliver seamless experiences across diverse screen types and browsers. Our meticulously crafted, fully responsive design guarantees a consistently smooth user experience, offering unmatched accessibility and convenience at every interaction.

  • Create Your Account or Log in

    Next, you have the choice to either create an account or log in to your existing account. By signing up or logging in, you unlock the potential for personalized interactions, access to your conversation history, and the ability to track your engagement with PSU-GPT. Alternatively, you may also opt to continue as a guest, allowing seamless access to PSU-GPT's functionalities without the need for account credentials.

  • Start Exploring

    You're all set to embark on a seamless interactive journey with PSU-GPT! Get ready to engage by typing your questions and receiving accurate responses instantly. Your feedback is incredibly valuable to us! Express your satisfaction with helpful answers using the "Positive Thumbs Up" button, where you can rate the response from Fair to Excellent and provide additional comments. Should you encounter any issues, don't hesitate to use the "Negative Thumbs Down" to help us improve PSU-GPT. We highly value your feedback as we continuously strive to enhance your experience. For any questions, our support team is here to assist you. Happy interacting!
